What's On

We are constantly in the process of developing stories we feel are vital to share and uplift.

Summer 2022

Bodyscapes

by Hannah Gerard

Running From 06/12 - 07/01

In an exploration of the body through unconventional mediums, Hannah Gerard tells the story of a recent cancer survivor as she attempts to reconnect with her body post-surgery. Starring North York's own Manuela Abbott, Bodyscapes is a visual monument.

The Last Solo Dance

by Tomi Eke

Running From 07/01 - 07/14

The world is set to end in 17 days and Kayode needs to prepare a dance on behalf of the human race in front of the gods to determine whether humans are worthy enough to be saved from destruction. This deeply human story is full of love, rage, and sharp humour.

Shadows In Reflection

by Sahar Jaziri

Running From 07/16 - 07/29

Saif’s shadow has detached from his body and has begun speaking to him from the netherworld. Saif’s shadow knows everything about him, and commands him to face himself over the guilt he feels about his troubled past. Can Saif rise above a tainted history?
