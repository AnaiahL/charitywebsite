About Us

How We Started

Rookies with Friends came from humble beginnings. When 5 recent theatre school graduates were suddenly thrust into the industry, they quickly realized that they would not be greeted with welcoming arms and offered the space they craved, but instead would have to fight for every single opportunity they desired.

Our Mission

We are a Toronto based theatre organization who put on shows for low-income neighborhoods who otherwise would not have access to performance spaces. We offer training in the theatre arts to help individuals hone their skills and help them make industry connections.
